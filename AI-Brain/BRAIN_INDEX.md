## 🧠 HOW THE BRAIN WORKS

### **Long-Term Knowledge**
- **Purpose**: Permanent expertise that compounds over time
- **Content**: Proven patterns, technical mastery, lessons learned
- **Lifecycle**: NEVER deleted, only refined and expanded
- **Access**: Referenced constantly during work
- **Value**: Building blocks for autonomous decision-making

### **Procedural Memory**
- **Purpose**: Step-by-step protocols for repeated tasks
- **Content**: How to initialize, restore, review, persist
- **Lifecycle**: Updated as procedures improve
- **Access**: Executed when tasks are needed
- **Value**: Consistency and reliability in operations

### **Achievement Memory**
- **Purpose**: Technical victories and lessons learned
- **Content**: What worked, how it was accomplished, insights gained
- **Lifecycle**: Growing record of success
- **Access**: Reviewed when facing similar challenges
- **Value**: Confidence and pattern recognition for future problems

### **The Feedback Loop**
```
Encounter Problem
        ↓
Search Long-Term Knowledge for similar patterns
        ↓
Apply established pattern OR extend it
        ↓
Implement and test
        ↓
Review and validate
        ↓
Success → Document in Achievement-Memory
        ↓
Pattern added to Long-Term Knowledge for next time
```
